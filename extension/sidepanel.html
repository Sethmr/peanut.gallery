Recording a podcast? By default Peanut Gallery plays captured audio back through your speakers so you hear the video normally. If your recording software (OBS, Riverside, etc.) or a virtual cable (BlackHole, VB-Audio) is already routing the audio, turn passthrough off to avoid doubling. You can also send passthrough to a specific output device your recording rig doesn’t capture.

On (default): you hear the video as normal. Off: tab is captured silently — use this if another app is already playing the audio.

passthrough

Route passthrough to a specific device — useful for sending it to monitor-only headphones or a virtual cable. Device names show after you start capture once.
