# ElevenLabs SFX prompt — Lon Harris ("The Reframe")

**Duration:** 0.8s
**Prompt influence:** 70

## Prompt

A cinematic pivot cue: a clean wooden film-slate clap on beat one (sharp attack, short wooden resonance), immediately followed by a smooth reverse-reverb whoosh rising and then pulling backward in time (as if rewinding a shot), ending on a soft vinyl-static whisper. Subtle tape saturation, slight analog warmth. No voice. Evokes a film editor cutting to a flashback — the sonic signature of "let me reframe this." Dry on the clap, short lush reverb on the whoosh only.
