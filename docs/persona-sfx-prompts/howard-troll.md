# ElevenLabs SFX prompt — The Troll (Howard Stern provocateur)

**Duration:** 0.8s
**Prompt influence:** 65

## Prompt

A vintage rotary-phone handset lifting off the cradle — sharp plastic click — followed immediately by a single payphone "bloop" dial tone at low pitch, then a very short, breathy, mischievous snicker tail (no intelligible words, just a wet "khh-heh" exhale). Lo-fi telephone EQ band-pass 300-3400 Hz. Slight tape hiss underneath. Feels like the opening beat of a 1990s prank call about to derail a live TV broadcast. No music, no reverb.
