# ElevenLabs SFX prompt — Molly Wood

**Duration:** 0.7s
**Prompt influence:** 70

## Prompt

A quick journalist's desk cue: one sharp mechanical typewriter keystrike (manual Olivetti-style, full metal-arm impact), immediately followed by a single page-flip rustle, capped by a soft brass desk-bell "ting" with a short 400ms decay. Clean acoustic recording, no reverb, close-mic'd. Brisk and precise — the whole cue lands in under a second. No voice. Evokes NPR-studio professionalism meeting a 1950s newsroom.
